UoM Graduation Ceremony

November 09, 2024 | Xiaoyi Meng | US

Living and studying in Manchester, especially as an international finance student at the Alliance Manchester Business School, was a transformative experience—quite different from my time in Cambridge. Cambridge, a quiet, small county town in the south near London, felt serene and studious. In contrast, Manchester buzzed with urban energy, its atmosphere often shadowed by gray, rainy skies, adding to its unique character.

My graduation ceremony remains one of my most unforgettable experiences—not because it marked the end of an important chapter in my life, but because of the profound words spoken by the principal that still resonate deeply with me. The speech of president taught me how to be at peace with myself and embrace life’s unpredictability:


"You know you will be treated unfairly, but that will teach you the value of justice. You will suffer betrayal, but that will teach you the importance of loyalty. You will feel lonely at times, but that will teach you never to take those around you for granted. And above all, you will never stop learning."

These words have become a guiding light, reminding me to find meaning and growth in life’s challenges.
